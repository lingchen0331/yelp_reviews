What makes a good business?¶

Chen Ling¶

2017/12/08¶

To begin with, there are millions of gigabytes every day are generated by blogs, social websites, and web pages. Companies are gathering all of these data for understanding users and their passions and give these reports to the companies to adjust their plans. Therefore, understanding these data is really important for the development of company.

Yelp.com is an great example. People can leave comments on restaurant's food, decoration, service, etc. However, what's in a review? Is it positive or negative? Do people have preference in certain area? And what are factors that a good restaurant has to have? This project aims at Natural Language Processing and Sentiment Analysis on Yelp's reviews.
